FAQ

Common Questions

What exactly is a Technical Virtual Assistant?+
A Technical Virtual Assistant is a remote professional who handles technical tasks across multiple domains — web development, automation, design, video, AI systems, and more. Unlike a general VA who handles scheduling and emails, a Technical VA can actually build, design, and automate things for your business. Think of me as a remote technical co-founder without the equity.
How can one person handle so many different skills?+
Over 5+ years I've deliberately built expertise across interconnected technical domains. Web dev, automation, design, video, and 3D are all part of the same technical ecosystem — they reinforce each other. I work on one project at a time per service area, ensuring quality over quantity. My workflow is also heavily assisted by AI tools that amplify output while maintaining quality.
What services do you specialize in most?+
My strongest specialties are: AI Automation (n8n, Make.com, Zapier, AI Agents), Web Development (HTML/CSS/JS + Supabase), and GoHighLevel (funnels, CRM, workflows). These are the areas where I have the deepest experience and most project history. Graphic design, video editing, CAD, and animation are strong secondary skills I've used in real client projects.
How long does a typical project take?+
It depends on scope. Simple automation workflows: 3–5 business days. Business websites: 1–2 weeks. Full web applications: 2–4 weeks. Design packages: 3–7 days. Video editing: 2–5 days per video. I always provide a clear, honest timeline before any work starts. No surprises.
Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?+
Absolutely none. Every deliverable comes with clear documentation, a walkthrough video, and support. You run your business — I handle the technical execution. If something needs to change after delivery, just tell me and I handle it.
Do you work with international clients?+
Yes — I work with clients globally. Based in Daet, Bicol, Philippines, I currently serve clients in the US, Australia, Canada, UK, and Southeast Asia. I'm available across timezones via WhatsApp, Calendly, email, or Slack. Communication is always clear, responsive, and async-friendly for international collaboration.
How are projects priced?+
All projects are fixed-price — no hourly billing surprises. Pricing is based on scope, complexity, and deliverables. I send a clear quote before any work begins. Book a free 30-minute Discovery Call and I'll give you an honest estimate within 24 hours of our conversation.
Can you handle ongoing support after a project is delivered?+
Yes. I offer ongoing retainer arrangements for clients who need continuous support — whether that's monthly updates to a website, managing automation systems, creating regular design assets, or acting as a part-time Technical VA. Many clients work with me on an ongoing basis across multiple skill areas.
